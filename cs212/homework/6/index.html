I want to solve a small problem that I will be turning away from personally (because of standard array), but often when creating a D&D character rolling stats can be time consuming so I want to make it simple. Basically the program will roll 6 stats 4d6 each and automatically sorts them in lowest to largest. Then it adds the highest 3 numbers and arbitraraly assigns them to a stat. They can be moved around but this was done just to make it feel more complete.
